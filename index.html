CRC operational intent

Build the acquired primary care model into a CRC-ready enterprise blueprint, then pilot, refine, and scale.
Recruit replacements and successors fast enough that rounding and regional burdens taper as enterprise work expands.
Use AHCA and government affairs as amplifiers of CRC outcomes, not stand-alone obligations.

King operational intent

Treat King as a stable academic platform with hard seasonal peaks, especially onsite term starts and late-April/early-May ceremonies.
Create a lightweight but durable grant opportunity process: weekly scan, living tracker, monthly digest, documented handoff logic.
Use King as the academic anchor for multi-site scholarship and policy credibility.

Leadership positioning intent

VUSN, Access to Care TN, SONSIEL, and JHU IPS should all tell one coherent story: advanced practice nursing leadership in care access, policy, and system design.
Every service role should yield at least one visible structural output and one reputation-building output.
Keep A4AL intentionally low-weight so it remains a board affiliation rather than a drag on execution.
